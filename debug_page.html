The Enclave plasma gun is a modular ranged weapon in Fallout 76 .

    • 1.1 Weapon modifications
  • 2 Locations

Characteristics [ ]

A modified plasma gun used by the Appalachian Enclave . It has a distinct black and silver chassis as well as opaque side ports with a cascading, oxidation-like cyan pattern in place of visible green plasma.

Compared to a normal plasma gun, the Enclave variant deals increased damage and is slightly more accurate.

Modding the gun can be tricky because only the prime receiver has a plan, all of the other mods can only be obtained via two means:

  1. Obtaining a gun with random mods.
  2. Enclave events have a 10% drop chance for a box mod
  3. Loose mods found in the world, or sold by vendors in the Cranberry Bog , the Mire , and only night vision scopes can be sold in the Savage Divide Note: the true flamer mod can only be sold in the Cranberry Bog.

Locations [ ]

  • Sold by the MODUS production terminal .
  • 30% chance of being awarded from Dropped Connection .
  • 50% chance of dropping during One of Us .
  • Carried by Scorched Enclave officers in the Enclave research facility .
  • Can be rewarded for claiming Wade Airport as a workshop .

Notes [ ]

  • Before December 2023, many mods for this gun were either unobtainable, or only spawned pre-attached on the weapon. Now, they all have loose mods.
  • This weapon is affected by the Grounded mutation.
  • Slain enemies may turn to goo piles. Enemies finished by a critical hit will always turn into goo.
  • Anything killed by plasma weapons has a chance to drop nuclear material on death. This chance increases if the target has been melted to goo.
  • Legendary variants can be obtained by applying a legendary module at a weapons workbench. They can also drop from legendary enemies or be purchased from the Purveyor , but only as a basic pistol.
